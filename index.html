About Nelton Cereals & Groceries

Established in 2019, Nelton Cereals & Groceries has grown to become a leading supplier of premium agricultural products to businesses across Kenya. We specialize in providing clean, sorted, and certified cereals and legumes to retailers, processors, and exporters.
